The Damage Role

Damage heroes focus on eliminating enemies and securing kills. They are key to winning team fights.

In Overwatch, DPS impact the game by creating pressure: winning angles, forcing enemy cooldowns, and finishing low-health targets. Good damage play isn’t only about raw aim — it’s also about positioning and timing.

About DPS Heroes Guide

DPS heroes specialize in offensive gameplay. Some focus on mobility like Tracer and Genji, while others specialize in range like Widowmaker.

This guide highlights common DPS hero strengths and gives practical strategies you can apply in matches, such as playing high ground, focusing targets, and using ultimates efficiently.

Use the DPS Heroes page to explore different playstyles, and the Strategies page to learn how to take fights smarter.
